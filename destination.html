Live your life through travel!

Live your life to the fullest by exploring new destinations, embracing adventures, and creating unforgettable memories.

Golden Triangle Tour (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur)

Embark on the iconic Golden Triangle Tour, connecting the rich histories of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. Marvel at Delhi’s Mughal-era monuments, experience the timeless romance of the Taj Mahal in Agra, and admire the royal grandeur of Jaipur’s palaces and forts. With bustling markets, colorful streets, and diverse cultural flavors, this journey showcases the essence of India’s heritage. It’s a perfect introduction for first-time visitors, combining history, architecture, and vibrant local life in one unforgettable circuit.

Kerala Backwaters & Beaches

Sail through the peaceful backwaters of Kerala aboard a traditional houseboat and embrace the natural beauty that surrounds you. From the coconut-lined lagoons of Alleppey to the pristine beaches of Varkala and Kovalam, Kerala offers a refreshing escape into tranquility. Indulge in Ayurvedic wellness, flavorful cuisine, and scenic landscapes that soothe the soul. Whether it’s a romantic getaway or a relaxing retreat, the charm of Kerala lies in its harmony between water, land, and culture.

Rating:
135

Himalayan Adventure

Experience the raw beauty and spiritual energy of the Himalayas through thrilling adventures and breathtaking vistas. From trekking in Manali and paragliding in Bir-Billing to exploring ancient monasteries in Ladakh and Sikkim, the Himalayas offer something for every traveler. Adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers alike are drawn to the snow-capped peaks, serene valleys, and challenging terrains. This journey is more than just physical — it’s a spiritual and emotional connection to nature and self.

Rating:
94

Serenity in Goa

Unwind in Goa, India’s most loved coastal paradise, where golden sands meet vibrant culture. Spend your days lounging on beaches like Baga or Palolem, exploring Portuguese-era churches, or enjoying the buzzing flea markets. Whether you're dancing the night away at beach parties or savoring seafood at seaside shacks, Goa offers something for every mood. Beyond the nightlife lies a serene side of hidden waterfalls, yoga retreats, and peaceful sunsets. It’s the perfect blend of leisure and adventure.

Rating:
78

Majestic Rajasthan

Step into a land of kings, camels, and color in majestic Rajasthan. Discover the romance of Udaipur’s lakes, the golden charm of Jaisalmer’s desert forts, and the grandeur of Jaipur’s royal heritage. With every city telling a story through its architecture, festivals, and traditional crafts, Rajasthan captures the soul of India’s royal past. Ride through sand dunes, explore bustling bazaars, and immerse yourself in folk music and dance. It’s a journey into a timeless, regal world.

Rating:
103

Mystical North-East India

Uncover the hidden treasures of North-East India, where nature, culture, and spirituality blend seamlessly. Wander through the living root bridges of Meghalaya, sip tea in Assam’s lush plantations, and discover the peaceful monasteries of Sikkim and Arunachal Pradesh. Known for its biodiversity and unique tribal cultures, this region feels like a different world within India. With untouched landscapes, clean towns, and warm hospitality, the North-East invites curious travelers to explore its serene and mystical allure.

Rating:
89

Most Popular Places

Explore the world's top travel spots loved by millions. Discover iconic destinations full of charm and wonder.

Paris

Paris enchants visitors with its timeless charm, world-famous landmarks, and romantic ambiance. Admire the Eiffel Tower, stroll along the Seine, and explore the art at the Louvre. Indulge in exquisite cuisine at sidewalk cafés and shop at chic boutiques in Champs-Élysées. From historic cathedrals to vibrant street life, every corner of Paris tells a story. Whether you're seeking art, fashion, or simply a magical experience, Paris promises elegance, culture, and unforgettable memories in the heart of France.

Rating:
124

Dubai

Dubai offers a unique blend of futuristic architecture, luxury shopping, and traditional Arabian culture. Witness the world’s tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, explore expansive malls, and venture into the golden dunes on a desert safari. Enjoy indoor skiing, waterfront dining at the Marina, and traditional souks filled with gold and spices. Whether you're seeking thrill, luxury, or cultural insight, Dubai dazzles with its energy, innovation, and year-round sunshine in the heart of the UAE.

Rating:
97

Manali

Manali is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Himachal Pradesh mountains, known for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventure sports. Roam the charming streets of Old Manali, visit the Hadimba Temple, or paraglide in Solang Valley. In winter, enjoy skiing and snowfall; in summer, explore apple orchards and scenic trails. Whether you’re looking for peace, romance, or thrill, Manali offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and mountain adventures all year round.

Rating:
75

Ooty

Ooty, located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, is a serene retreat known for its cool climate, tea plantations, and colonial charm. Ride the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, stroll through the vibrant botanical gardens, and cruise across Ooty Lake. With misty hills, eucalyptus forests, and a peaceful vibe, Ooty offers a refreshing escape from city life. Ideal for families, honeymooners, and nature lovers, it’s a soothing blend of scenic beauty and relaxed southern hospitality.

Rating:
61

Trending Now!

Uncover the hottest travel destinations everyone’s talking about. Book your next adventure before the crowds do!

Bali

Bali enchants with its lush landscapes, vibrant culture, and spiritual essence. Discover ancient temples like Uluwatu, relax on beaches in Seminyak or Nusa Dua, and trek through rice terraces in Ubud. Whether you're surfing the waves, diving into coral-rich waters, or enjoying a traditional Balinese spa, this island blends nature and tradition beautifully. With warm hospitality, lively art scenes, and delicious cuisine, Bali offers the perfect escape for couples, solo travelers, and families alike.

Rating:
88

Goa

Goa is a vibrant coastal getaway known for its golden beaches, lively shacks, and rich Portuguese heritage. Lounge on Anjuna Beach, dance the night away in Baga, or explore old churches and forts in Panjim. Taste spicy seafood, shop in colorful markets, or simply soak in the sunset by the Arabian Sea. From peaceful yoga retreats to high-energy nightlife, Goa caters to every traveler with its carefree charm, tropical vibe, and cultural depth.

Rating:
84

Maldives

The Maldives is a dreamy collection of coral islands in the Indian Ocean, famous for its overwater villas, turquoise waters, and luxury resorts. Snorkel in crystal-clear lagoons, enjoy private beach dinners, or unwind with a spa session above the waves. Ideal for honeymoons and peaceful retreats, this island nation offers unmatched serenity, marine life, and natural beauty. The Maldives is where barefoot luxury meets tranquility, making it a once-in-a-lifetime tropical escape.

Rating:
102

Agra

Agra is a historic city that proudly houses one of the world’s most iconic monuments — the Taj Mahal. Walk through Mughal gardens, explore the majestic Agra Fort, and learn about centuries of imperial history. With marble artistry, bustling local bazaars, and traditional sweets like petha, Agra blends architectural brilliance with cultural richness. Whether for history, romance, or photography, a visit to Agra offers a captivating glimpse into India’s royal and artistic legacy.

Rating:
56
